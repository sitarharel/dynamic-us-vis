The United States of America

The United States has 320 million people spread across 50 states, and certainly no two states are alike. This visualization allows the viewer to explore each state and the differences across all of them, from typical values like population and income, to more interesting metrics like the number of Starbucks stores. Data has been pooled from the U.S. Census Bureau, National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism, Fair Vote, The Measure of America, Death Penalty Information Center, National Center for Education Statistics, Starbucks, and more.
