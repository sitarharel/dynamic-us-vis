The United States of America

The United States has 320 million people spread across 50 states, and certainly no two states are alike. Use this visualization to explore each state and the differences across all of them, from typical values like population and income, to more interesting metrics like the number of Starbucks stores. Select the white dropdown menu to change the data shown and press one of the five buttons on the very bottom to see a different visualization. Hover or click on states for even more specifics. Try to find correlations between data sets in your favorite of the two graph modes! Data has been pooled from the U.S. Census Bureau, National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism, Fair Vote, The Measure of America, Death Penalty Information Center, National Center for Education Statistics, Starbucks, and more.
